How to Play DUSAR

📋 Game Setup

• 4 players in 2 teams (diagonal partners)

• 13 cards per player from standard deck

💰 Bidding Phase (After 5 cards dealt)

• Player 1 bids 9-13 or passes

• Each player can bid ONCE (higher than previous)

• Cannot bid twice - only Pass or higher bid

• Highest bidder chooses Trump suit

🎴 Card Rankings

• A (Ace) > K > Q > J > 10 > 9 > 8 > 7 > 6 > 5 > 4 > 3 > 2

• Trump 2 = SUPER CARD (beats everything!)

• Trump suit beats all other cards

📍 Trick Rules

• Must follow suit if you have it

• Trump beats non-trump

• Highest card wins the trick

🏆 Scoring

• Bid 9 & get 9+ = Team gets 9 points

• Bid 9 & get 6 = Opponents get 12 (double!)

• First to 100+ points WINS
